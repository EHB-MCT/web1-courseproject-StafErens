Punching Boros to Oblivion

In the fight against Boros, Saitama used a "Serious Punch" that was strong enough to neutralize a planet-destroying energy blast and obliterate Boros.

Jumping Back from the Moon in Seconds

During his fight with Boros, Saitama was knocked to the Moon and leaped back to Earth in an instant. Also survived on the Moon without any equipment or difficulty, showcasing resistance to the vacuum of space.
